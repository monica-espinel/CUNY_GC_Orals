Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil 1962–1989 Birthplace and early career. Began with performance art and pioneered holopoetry (holographic poetry). Engaged with Brazilian avant-garde movements and concrete poetry traditions. Holopoems (1983–1993)
Chicago, USA 1989–present Moved to the United States to pursue graduate studies. Developed telepresence and bioart practices. The proximity to major research institutions enabled his collaborations with scientists on transgenic art projects. Ornitorrinco (1989–1998), Genesis (1999), GFP Bunny (2000), Natural History of the Enigma (2003–2008)
International 1990s–present Exhibited globally at major venues and biennials. His work in space (Inner Telescope aboard ISS) represents the expansion of his practice beyond Earth. Inner Telescope (2017)
