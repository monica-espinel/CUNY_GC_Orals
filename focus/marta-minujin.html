Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Buenos Aires, Argentina 1943–present (primary residence) Birthplace and lifelong home. The Instituto Torcuato Di Tella (ITDT) was the epicenter of Argentine avant-garde in the 1960s, where she produced seminal happenings. Post-dictatorship Buenos Aires inspired her public monuments addressing democracy and freedom. La Menesunda (1965), El Obelisco de pan dulce (1979), El Partenón de Libros (1983)
Paris, France Early 1960s Formative period exposed her to Nouveaux Réalistes (Christo, Niki de Saint Phalle, Jean Tinguely). This fostered her commitment to breaking traditional art boundaries and embracing life as art. La Destrucción (1963)
New York, USA 1960s–1970s (periods) Collaborated with leading figures of the American avant-garde including Andy Warhol and Allan Kaprow. Exposure to pop art and happening culture. Minuphone (1967), Paying Off the Argentine Foreign Debt with Corn (1985)
Washington, D.C., USA 1980s Site of collaborative projects addressing international relations through art. Paying Off the Argentine Foreign Debt with Corn (with Andy Warhol, 1985)
Kassel, Germany 2017 Documenta 14 provided international platform to recreate her banned books monument as a statement against censorship. The Parthenon of Books (2017)

Major Life Events

Year Event Location
1943 Born Buenos Aires, Argentina
Early 1960s Studies and lives in Paris; encounters Nouveaux Réalistes Paris
1962 Begins making soft sculptures from mattresses (Colchones series) Buenos Aires/Paris
1963 Creates La Destrucción – first major happening Paris
1964 Creates ¡Revuélquese y Viva! participatory mattress installation Buenos Aires
1965 Creates La Menesunda – groundbreaking multi-sensory environment Buenos Aires
1967 Creates Minuphone – interactive telephone booth installation New York
1979 Creates El Obelisco de pan dulce – edible monument Buenos Aires
1983 Creates first El Partenón de Libros – monument of banned books celebrating return of democracy Buenos Aires
1985 Collaborates with Andy Warhol on Paying Off the Argentine Foreign Debt with Corn New York
2017 Recreates The Parthenon of Books at Documenta 14 Kassel, Germany

Key Works Timeline

Year Artwork Medium Location Context Image
1962 Colchones Mattresses, soft sculpture Buenos Aires/Paris – soft sculptures from mattresses and blankets
