Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Brooklyn, New York, USA 1934–present Birthplace (to Puerto Rican family) and lifelong base. The urban environment and Puerto Rican community in New York shaped his exploration of cultural identity and institutional critique. Founded El Museo del Barrio to serve Latinx communities. Archaeological Finds series (1960s), Piano Destruction Concert (1966–67)
London, UK 1966 Participated in the Destruction in Art Symposium (DIAS), a key international gathering of destructivist artists. This cemented his role in the global Destruction Art movement. DIAS performances and presentations
East Harlem, New York 1969–present Founded El Museo del Barrio in the heart of the Puerto Rican community, explicitly challenging the exclusion of Latinx artists from mainstream institutions. Museum founding, community art education initiatives
