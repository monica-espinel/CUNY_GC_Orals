Andres de Santa Maria

Andrés de Santa María (1860–1945) was a pioneering Colombian artist who introduced Impressionism to Colombia and significantly shaped the direction of modern Colombian art. His life and career were marked by extensive periods spent in Europe, especially France and Belgium, which deeply influenced his style.
