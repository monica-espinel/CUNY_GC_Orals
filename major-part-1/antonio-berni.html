Antonio Berni

Antonio Berni (1905–1981) was one of Argentina's most influential 20th-century artists, renowned for his powerful social commentary and innovative technique. His work evolved from Surrealism to Social Realism, culminating in his iconic Juanito Laguna series featuring mixed-media collages made from found materials.
