Débora Arango

Débora Arango (1907–2005) is regarded as one of Colombia's most daring and original 20th-century artists, celebrated for challenging societal norms and fearless portrayal of taboo subjects. A pioneer of expressionism in Colombian art, she became the first woman in Colombia to publicly exhibit female nudes, and her work addresses themes of political violence, prostitution, and social injustice.
