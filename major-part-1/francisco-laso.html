Francisco Laso

Francisco Laso (1823–1869) was a pivotal Peruvian painter and politician, regarded as a precursor to the indigenismo movement in Latin American art. His solemn, respectful depictions of indigenous figures marked the beginning of indigenismo, connecting art to emerging nationalist and cultural discourses amidst Peru's post-independence search for identity.
