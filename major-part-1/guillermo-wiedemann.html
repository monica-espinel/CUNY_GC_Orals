Guillermo Wiedemann

Guillermo Wiedemann (1905–1969) was a German-born artist who became one of the leading figures in Colombian modern art. Fleeing Nazi persecution of modernist art in 1939, he settled in Colombia where the tropical light and vibrant colors transformed his style from German Expressionism to abstract painting that captured the essence of the Colombian landscape.
