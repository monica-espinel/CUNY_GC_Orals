José María Velasco

José María Velasco (1840–1912) was a 19th-century Mexican artist celebrated as one of the greatest landscape painters in Latin American history. His panoramic depictions of the Valley of Mexico set a new standard for landscape painting and contributed to Mexico's national identity during a time of modernization and political transformation.
