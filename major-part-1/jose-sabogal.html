José Sabogal

José Sabogal (1888–1956) is regarded as the foremost leader of Peru's indigenist art movement and a central figure in Peruvian muralism. Though not of indigenous descent himself, he staunchly promoted Andean, pre-Columbian aesthetics and the dignity of indigenous Peruvians, founding what became known as the "Peruvian School" of painting.
