Manuel Álvarez Bravo

Manuel Álvarez Bravo (1902–2002) was a pioneering Mexican photographer, celebrated as one of the most influential figures in 20th-century Latin American photography. His work is noted for its poetic images of Mexican people and landscapes, blending modernist, surrealist, and distinctly Mexican sensibilities. Though connected to Surrealism, he remained focused on Mexican roots and humanist themes.
