Ramón Torres Méndez

Ramón Torres Méndez (1809–1885) was a renowned Colombian painter, lithographer, caricaturist, and miniaturist, considered one of the leading figures of costumbrismo in 19th-century Colombia. His meticulous illustrations provide documentary-like insights into Colombian society, depicting everyday life, customs, social types, and occupations of his era.
