Xul Solar

Xul Solar (Oscar Agustín Alejandro Schulz Solari, 1887–1963) was a pioneering Argentine artist celebrated for his visionary esoteric art, deep interest in mysticism, and profound engagement with languages and astrology. Close friend of Jorge Luis Borges, he created invented languages like Neocriollo and Panlengua, designed his own Tarot cards, and produced mystical paintings filled with cosmic symbolism.
