Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Cartagena, Colombia 1945 (birth) Birthplace. Early exposure to Caribbean culture and Colombian coastal identity. Early formative period
Barranquilla, Colombia 1945–present (primary residence) Considers Barranquilla his hometown, having lived there since infancy. The city's cultural scene and proximity to Caribbean influences shaped his engagement with popular culture and comics. Associated with the Barranquilla Group literary movement. Ambient Spaces (1968), Grabados populares series, Dick Tracy-inspired drawings
Perugia and Venice, Italy Art history studies period Studied art history in Italy. Exposure to European art traditions and Renaissance masters influenced his appropriation strategies and engagement with art historical references. Collages and engravings referencing European masters
