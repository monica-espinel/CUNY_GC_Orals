Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil 1964–present (birth and primary residence) Birthplace and home. Studied at Escola de Artes Visuais do Parque Lage. Influenced by Brazilian Neo-Concrete movement's emphasis on viewer participation and blurring boundaries between art and life. Collaboration with Huni Kuin indigenous community shaped spiritual dimensions of work. Nave (various), early biomorphic sculptures
Paris, France Various exhibitions Major installations at prestigious Paris venues established international recognition. Engagement with European institutions expanded reach of participatory practice. Léviathan Thot (Panthéon), Nosso Barco Tambor Terra (Grand Palais, 2025)
New York, USA Various exhibitions Exhibited at major New York institutions including Park Avenue Armory. International visibility reinforced his position as leading Brazilian contemporary artist. Anthropodino (Park Avenue Armory)
Zurich, Switzerland 2018 Created monumental installation at Zurich main station, bringing participatory art to public transit space. GaiaMotherTree
