Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Santiago, Chile 1943–present (birth and primary residence) Birthplace and lifelong home. Became central figure in the Avanzada, an influential group critiquing Pinochet dictatorship through conceptual art. Political isolation and censorship led to development of Airmail Paintings as method to participate in global art discourse. Pinturas Aeropostales (Airmail Paintings) series (1983–present)
Europe 1962–1969 (studies) Studied painting and printmaking in Europe. Exposure to European avant-garde techniques influenced his later synthesis of painting, photography, drawing, and text. Early painting and printmaking works
