Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Mexico City, Mexico 1943–1968, 1974–2001, 2006–2017 Birthplace (Tlacopac). Studied under Mexican modernist masters. Political climate of Tlatelolco massacre (1968) led to exile. After returning in 1974, co-founded Grupo Proceso Pentágono and H2O Talleres de Comunicación for collaborative performance and mural painting. La Montaña (1965), Dibujos y Epoxis (1965), group murals
Devon, England 1968–1974 (exile) Exiled after Tlatelolco massacre. Founded Beau Geste Press with David Mayor and Martha Hellion, producing over 150 experimental publications. Central to Fluxus movement and international mail art networks. Beau Geste Press publications (1970s), artist books with Ulises Carrión
Brazil 2001–2006 (cultural attaché) Served as Mexico's cultural attaché in Brazil. Continued artistic exchanges and cultural diplomacy. Cultural diplomacy and exhibitions
