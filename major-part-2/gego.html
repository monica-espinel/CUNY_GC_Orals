Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Hamburg, Germany 1912 (birth) Birthplace. Born to a Jewish family. Forced by Nazi persecution to emigrate in 1939. Early formative period
Stuttgart, Germany 1932–1938 (studies) Studied architecture and engineering at Technische Hochschule Stuttgart. Training provided foundation in structural thinking, spatial relationships, and material properties that would define her sculptural practice. Engineering and architectural foundation
Caracas, Venezuela 1939–1994 (primary residence until death) Emigrated to escape Nazi persecution. Early years involved architectural and furniture design work. Became naturalized Venezuelan citizen. Began artistic career in 1950s when geometric abstraction and kinetic art were prominent. Taught at Venezuelan institutions, influencing generations of artists. Reticulárea (1969–), Drawings Without Paper, geometric sculptures

Key Works Timeline

Year Artwork Medium Location Context Image
