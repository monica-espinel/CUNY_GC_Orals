Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Ciudad Bolívar, Venezuela 1923 (birth) Birthplace. Began artistic career painting posters for local cinemas. Early exposure to visual representation in public contexts. Early work, foundation
Caracas, Venezuela 1942–1950 (studies) Studied at Escuela de Artes Plásticas y Aplicadas. Exposure to European avant-garde movements through reproductions sparked interest in Cubism and geometric abstraction. Early paintings
Paris, France 1950–2005 (primary residence) Moved to Paris and joined vibrant artistic community including Victor Vasarely, Yaacov Agam, and Jean Tinguely. Participated in seminal "Le Mouvement" exhibition (1955) at Galerie Denise René. Developed kinetic and Op Art practice exploring movement, optical effects, and viewer participation. Penetrables, Vibraciones, serial works
