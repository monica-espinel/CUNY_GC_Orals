Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Lübeck, Germany 1937 (birth) Birthplace. Family emigrated to Uruguay in 1939. Early formative period
Montevideo, Uruguay 1939–1964 (upbringing and studies) Raised and educated in Uruguay. Studied sculpture and architecture at Escuela Nacional de Bellas Artes. Latin American political context shaped political consciousness and critical perspective. Early work, education
Munich, Germany Studies period Studied at Akademie der Bildenden Künste. Exposure to European artistic traditions. European artistic training
New York, USA 1964–present Moved to New York; co-founded New York Graphic Workshop with Liliana Porter and José Guillermo Castillo. Reimagined printmaking's social impact. Developed language-based conceptual works critiquing institutions and political violence. This Is a Mirror, You Are a Written Sentence (1966–68), Leftovers (1970), Uruguayan Torture Series (1983–84)
