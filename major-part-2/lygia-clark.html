Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Belo Horizonte, Brazil 1920 (birth) Birthplace in Minas Gerais state. Early formative period
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil 1940s–1968, 1976–1988 Studied painting with Roberto Burle Marx. Became deeply involved in Constructivist and Neo-Concrete movements. Co-founded Neo-Concretism promoting subjective, organic, participatory approaches. Returned from Paris in 1976 to develop therapeutic practice. Bichos (1960s), early geometric paintings, therapeutic sessions
Paris, France 1950–1952 (studies), 1968–1976 Studied with Isaac Dobrinsky, Fernand Léger, and Arpad Szenes. Returned to Paris 1968–1976 to teach and develop sensory objects and therapeutic practice. Established art therapy sessions using "relational objects." Nostalgia do Corpo series, sensory objects, Caminhando (1963)

Year Artwork Medium Location Context Image
