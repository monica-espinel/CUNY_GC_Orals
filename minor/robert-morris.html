Places Lived and Stylistic Influence

Place Period Stylistic Relationship Key Artworks
Kansas City, Missouri 1931–1950s Birthplace. Early interests in engineering, dance, and theater. Early life
San Francisco Late 1950s Studied at San Francisco Art Institute. Worked with dancer Simone Forti (first wife). Involved in avant-garde dance and performance. Early performance works
New York City 1961–2018 Became leading Minimalist sculptor. Created geometric plywood and metal sculptures emphasizing viewer's spatial experience. Wrote influential texts including "Notes on Sculpture" (1966) and "Anti-Form" (1968). Later explored felt sculptures, Process Art, and Land Art. Participated in Judson Dance Theater. Untitled (L-Beams) (1965), Untitled (Box with Sound) (1961), Felt pieces (1967–68), Continuous Project Altered Daily (1969)
